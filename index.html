About Me

Am I Young? Yes, I am 18 years old and i'm turning 19 in April. I am from Lebanon and I speak arabic.

I chose to study in the Graphic Design Program.

Why?

Because I want to learn motion graphics and improve my design skills. I've always been interested in it since I was younger and my aunt inspired me to be one because she was learning graphic design at the time.

My aunt gave me some advice back then and it's something I still remember to this day.

"My Graphic Design professors where very strict about our projects that if they didn't like them, they would rip our project in front of us and tell us to redo it. My advice to you is to not get sad if your teachers don't like your work, they are preparing you for the real world."

- Aunt
